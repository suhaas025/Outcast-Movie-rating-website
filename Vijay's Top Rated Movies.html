Vijay's Top Rated Movies

by IMDb-Editors | last updated - 19 Jun 2020

We celebrate the actor, known by his fans as Thalapathy Vijay, on his birthday with a look back
at his top rated performances.

1. Kaththi (2014)

Not Rated | 166 min | Action, Drama


A case of mistaken identity embroils an escaped convict in a fight
against a large corporation intent on seizing a village's land.

Director: A.R. Murugadoss | Stars: Thalapathy Vijay, Samantha Ruth Prabhu,
Neil Nitin Mukesh, Tota Roy Chowdhury

Votes: 26,066

2. Thuppakki (2012)

Not Rated | 165 min | Action, Thriller


An army captain is on a mission to track down and destroy a
terrorist gang and deactivate the sleeper cells under its
command.

Director: A.R. Murugadoss | Stars: Thalapathy Vijay, Kajal Aggarwal, Vidyut
Jammwal, Sathyan

Votes: 33,411

3. Mersal (2017)

Not Rated | 172 min | Action, Thriller


A detective arrests a doctor for crimes targeting medical
professionals but later finds the real culprit in a tale of revenge,
corruption and magic.

Director: Atlee Kumar | Stars: Thalapathy Vijay, Nithya Menen, S.J. Suryah,
Kajal Aggarwal

Votes: 33,206

4. Ghilli (2004)

Not Rated | 160 min | Action, Drama, Romance


Velu, an aspiring kabaddi player, goes to Madurai to participate in

Director: Dharani | Stars: Thalapathy Vijay, Trisha Krishnan, Prakash Raj,
Ashish Vidyarthi

Votes: 14,159

5. Nanban (2012)

Not Rated | 188 min | Comedy, Drama


10 years after leaving college, two best friends and their rival go

Director: S. Shankar | Stars: Thalapathy Vijay, Jiiva, Srikanth, Ileana D'Cruz

Votes: 15,116

6. Pokkiri (2007)

Not Rated | 161 min | Action, Crime, Thriller


Thamizh is a killer for hire with no ties and who will do any dirty
job for money. He has a girlfriend who loves him but also detests
his violent nature. But is Thamizh really what he purports to be?

Director: Prabhu Deva | Stars: Thalapathy Vijay, Asin Thottumkal, Prakash
Raj, Vadivelu

Votes: 12,397

7. Theri (2016)

Not Rated | 157 min | Action, Crime, Thriller


When his daughter's life is endangered by a local gang, Joseph
Kuruvilla will do whatever it takes to stop them. However, at the

Director: Atlee Kumar | Stars: Thalapathy Vijay, Samantha Ruth Prabhu, Amy
Jackson, Baby Nainika

Votes: 19,395

8. Bigil (2019)

Votes: 19,099

10. Kaavalan (2011)

Not Rated | 155 min | Action, Comedy, Drama


Meera starts calling bhoomi, her bodyguard just to fool him by
telling him she loves him. Problems arise when bhoomi falls in

Directors: Siddique, K.R. Gowri Shankar | Stars: Thalapathy Vijay, Asin

11. Thulladha Manamum Thullum (1999)

Kutty is a good-hearted soul, but unfortunate incidents tend to
